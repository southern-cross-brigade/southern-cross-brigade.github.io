Due to COVID-19, the 2021-22 Southern Cross Brigade to Cuba has been postponed. We plan for the 2022-23 brigade to proceed. Dates and further details for the 2022-23 brigade will be posted to this website in early 2022. If you would like to join the 2022-23 brigade, please email southerncrossbrigade@gmail.com to register your interest and we will keep you up to date!

Below you can read the information from our most recent brigade to get a taste of what it's like!

The 37th Southern Cross Brigade will start on 28 December 2019 and finish on 17 January 2020. A portion of the three week trip will be spent in a camp about an hour’s drive outside of Havana. This year’s program will also give participants an opportunity to spend time in Havana and to travel to the city of Santiago de Cuba in the south-east of the island and also to Holguín Province in the east of Cuba.

The 2019-20 Southern Cross Brigade program can be found here.

The program is subject to minor changes due to weather and other unforeseeable events. The purpose of the brigade is to actively participate and engage in activities and brigadistas are expected to attend all scheduled activities in the program.

If you are interested in attending the 37th annual Southern Cross Brigade to Cuba, please fill out a registration form and contact us.
