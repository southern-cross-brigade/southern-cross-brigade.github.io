The 37th Southern Cross Brigade will start on 28 December 2019 and finish on 17 January 2020. The majority of the three week trip will be spent in a camp about an hour’s drive outside of Havana. This year’s program will also give participants an opportunity to spend time in Havana and to travel from Havana, on the west side of the island, to the city of Santiago de Cuba in the south-east of the island and also to Holguín Province in the east of Cuba.

The upcoming program for the 37th Southern Cross Brigade 2019-20 will be posted to this page in April. To get an idea of the kinds of activities that the brigade offers, please view the program from the most recent 2018-19 Southern Cross Brigade here.

The program is subject to minor changes due to weather and other unforeseeable events. The purpose of the brigade is to actively participate and engage in activities and brigadistas are expected to attend all scheduled activities in the program.

If you are interested in attending the 37th annual Southern Cross Brigade to Cuba, please fill out a registration form and contact us.
